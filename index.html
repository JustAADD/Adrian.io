Yoo-na Ecommerce Website

Back-end Developer: Adrian Rodrigo Makiling
Front-end Developer: Jezzyrel Sanchez
Documents: Melvin Lomboy

Tools Used:

Information and Objectives:

YOO-NAA is an online apparel store business that was established on January 18, 2022, by
Ms. Andrea Delgado. It is a fashion retailing brand with many social media channels, including
Facebook, where it has 17,000 followers. YOO-NAA connects suppliers to their flexible chain through
on-demand manufacturing technology, allowing them to reduce inventory waste and deliver a
diverse selection of products to their consumers at competitive and cheap costs.

The Objectives of this Website aims to integrate a functioning website with admin portal,
customer portal, order placement, security through Gmail verification, customer order history, customer details, customer registration, database management, inventory, sales and
earning generation, mode of payment through PayPal rest API, Gcash QR code,
admin dashboard, and feedback.

Development Process:

Project methodology used: Agile Methodology

A flexible and adaptive approach to software development emphasizing customer satisfaction,
iterative delivery, and quick response to change. Comprises phases of Requirement Analysis,
Designing and Coding, Testing, Release, and Feedback, facilitating a swift and adaptable
development process.
